Window Cleaning

The service includes:
  • Exterior window cleaning with purified water using water-fed pole technology - up to 4th floor.
  • All the necessary equipment and detergents are provided by us.
  • PVC and aluminum frames will be cleaned as part of the service
  • There are no hidden costs with us!
  • We can take care of sticker removal upon request.
Please be advised:
  • We can reach up to the 4th floor.
  • Sometimes it's very hard to describe windows over the phone or email, which is why the technician will confirm the price on site.
  • The water-fed pole cleaning systems are attached to the vehicles, which means that we'll need a parking space within 100 feet of your property.
  • We strive to be on time for each service, but obviously traffic conditions can influence this. We'll always contact you in case of a change in the arrival time frame.
  • In cases of heavy rain or snow, we might need to re-arrange the appointment to ensure better results.
  • If you need to postpone or cancel your appointment, kindly give us a 24 h notice.
